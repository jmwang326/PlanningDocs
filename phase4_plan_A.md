# Phase 3: Vendor Plan A - The Lean Model (1-Entity)

## Project Overview

This project involves the creation of a single California Limited Liability Company ("the Company") to serve as a combined investment and operating vehicle. The Company will be taxed as a partnership.

*   **Entity:** `HAL2025, LLC`
*   **Ownership:**
    *   99% by the Founder's Trust (Capital Member)
    *   1% by a Children's Trust (Profits Interest Member)
*   **Purpose:** The Company will receive an initial capital contribution to fund an in-house R&D project in the AI/ML space. It will also employ the Founder.

---

## Vendor Roles & Scope

## 1. Legal Counsel

*   **Vendor Type:** Boutique corporate law firm specializing in LLC formation.
*   **Your Role:** To provide all legal services required to form and structure the Company correctly.
*   **Scope of Work:**
    1.  **Entity Formation:** File Articles of Organization for `HAL2025, LLC` with the CA Secretary of State and obtain an EIN.
    2.  **Governing Documents:** Draft a simple Operating Agreement reflecting the 99% / 1% ownership split and defining the rights of the capital member vs. the profits interest member.
    3.  **Gifting:** Draft legal documents to execute and document the gift of the 1% profits interest to the Children's Trust, ensuring it is structured to be defensible for gift tax purposes.

*   **Estimated Cost:** $3,000 - $7,000

## 2. Tax & Accounting

*   **Vendor Type:** CPA firm with experience in partnership taxation (Form 1065).
*   **Your Role:** To ensure the Company is tax-compliant and that all transactions are recorded correctly.
*   **Scope of Work:**
    1.  **Initial Setup:** Advise on the chart of accounts for bookkeeping.
    2.  **Annual Compliance:** Prepare and file the annual partnership tax return (Form 1065) and issue K-1s to the two trust members.
    3.  **Gift Tax:** Prepare and file the Form 709 gift tax return for the year the 1% interest is gifted to the Children's Trust.

*   **Estimated Annual Cost:** $2,000 - $5,000

## 3. Payroll & HR

*   **Vendor Type:** PEO (Professional Employer Organization) like Gusto or Rippling.
*   **Your Role:** To act as the payroll and benefits administrator for the Company.
*   **Scope of Work:**
    1.  **Onboarding:** Set up `HAL2025, LLC` as the employer and onboard the Founder as the first employee.
    2.  **Administration:** Process payroll, handle all tax withholdings and filings, and issue a W-2 to the Founder.
    3.  **Benefits:** Provide access to benefits such as health insurance and a 401(k) plan.

*   **Estimated Annual Cost:** $600 - $1,500

## Founder's Role & Action Timeline
### Founder's Roles
*   **As Manager of Founder's Trust:** You will direct the trustee to make all capital contributions (both Common and Preferred) to the LLC.
*   **As Manager of HAL2025 R&D, LLC:** You will hold ultimate management authority via the Voting units.
*   **As Employee of HAL2025 R&D, LLC:** You will be the key employee responsible for executing the R&D. You will sign a formal Employment Agreement and receive a W-2 salary.

### Trustee Action Items
*   **Trustee of Founder's Trust:**
    1.  Execute wire transfers for both the initial Common Unit co-investment and the subsequent Preferred Unit R&D funding.
    2.  Receive and safeguard membership certificates for 20% of Voting Common Units and 100% of Voting Preferred Units.
*   **Trustees of Children's Trusts (x2):**
    1.  Execute wire transfer for the initial Common Unit co-investment.
    2.  Receive and safeguard the membership certificate for 40% of Non-Voting Common Units.
